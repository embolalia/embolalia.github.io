While watching The Chosen a while back I saw their take on the <span class="jc bible">give to Caesar what is Caesar's</span> bit, and it made me think differently about our own money. Washington is just our attempt to build a better Caesar, after all. And in whose god do we trust? My God? Your God? [Jerry Falwell's God](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MyE5wjc4XOw)? Allah, Odin, Inanna? Certainly some of us trust Santa more than any other god. In the spirit of weird-ass evangelism I scribbled on a $1 bill to spread my point with the good people (or, more likely, never give out because ew).

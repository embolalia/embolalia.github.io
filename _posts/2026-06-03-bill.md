While watching The Chosen a while back I saw their take on the <span class="jc bible">give to Caesar what is Caesar's</span> bit. In it, he calls the inscription that calls Caesar the son of a god <span class=jc>meaningless</span>, and it made me think about our own money. Washington achieved [apotheosis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Apotheosis_of_Washington), after all. He, and the presidents that followed in the model he made, is our Caesar. And much of our money has a meaningless inscription, too: "In God We Trust". In whose god do we trust? My God? Your God? [Jerry Falwell's God](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MyE5wjc4XOw)? Allah, Odin, Inanna? Washington himself? Is Santa a god? In the spirit of weird-ass evangelism I scribbled on a $1 bill to spread my point with the good people (or, more likely, never give out because ew).
